EXTRACTIVE TEXT SUMMARIZATION WITH APPROACH TEXTUAL ENTAILMENT USING DEEP LEARNING

Extractive Text Summarization is a document reduction technique that selects a group of important sentences from the original document to form a summary. This technique is based on understanding sentences and summarizing by extracting important sentences and putting them together to form a summary that contains the essence of the original document. The goal of extractive text summarization is to save time and effort in reading the original document, which may be very long and complex.

Using a deep learning-based Textual Entailment approach, Extractive Text Summarization can become more effective in producing accurate and relevant summaries of the original document by considering the semantic relationships between sentences in the original document.

      TEXTUAL ENTAILMENT

      Textual Entailment is the ability of a sentence or text to convey information that requires knowledge or additional information from another sentence or text. In Text Summarization, Textual Entailment is used to understand the relationships between sentences in the original document and to extract the most important sentences to form a summary.
